Import from a photo

Importing Recipes · 2 min read

Photograph a recipe from a cookbook, magazine, or handwritten card and import it into ReciMe.

Steps

  1. Open ReciMe and tap the + button.
  2. Select Camera.
  3. Take a photo of the recipe, or pick an existing photo from your library.
  4. The photo appears on screen. Tap and hold on any text in the photo — iOS automatically recognises the text (Live Text).
  5. Select all the recipe text, tap Copy, then paste it into the text box below the photo.
  6. Tap Parse Recipe. ReciMe structures the ingredients and steps automatically.
📱 iOS Live Text: On iPhone XS and later running iOS 15+, you can tap and hold directly on text in any photo to select and copy it. This works perfectly for recipe books and printed recipes.
ℹ️ Best results: Make sure the photo is well-lit and the text is clear. Hold the phone parallel to the page to avoid distortion.
