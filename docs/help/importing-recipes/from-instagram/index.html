Import from Instagram

Importing Recipes · 2 min read

Save any recipe you find on Instagram — posts, Reels, and Stories — directly into your ReciMe cookbook in seconds.

Option 1 — Share directly from Instagram (fastest)

  1. Open the Instagram post or Reel that contains the recipe.
  2. Tap the Share (paper plane) icon below the post.
  3. Scroll down and tap More if ReciMe isn't visible.
  4. Select ReciMe from the app list.
  5. ReciMe opens automatically and begins importing the recipe.
💡 Tip: Add ReciMe to your Share Shortcuts for one-tap access. Go to the share sheet → scroll right to the app icons → tap the … button → select Edit and pin ReciMe to the front.

Option 2 — Paste the caption text

If the recipe is written in the Instagram caption (most food creators do this):

  1. Tap … on the post → Copy Link or long-press the caption to copy it.
  2. Open ReciMe and tap the + button.
  3. Select Paste Text.
  4. Paste the caption. ReciMe automatically detects ingredients and steps.
ℹ️ Note: Instagram doesn't publicly share structured recipe data, so results work best when the creator writes the full recipe in the caption.
