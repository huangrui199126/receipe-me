Import from Pinterest

Importing Recipes · 2 min read

Save recipes from Pinterest pins. ReciMe automatically follows the link to the original recipe website and imports the full recipe with ingredients and steps.

Option 1 — Share from Pinterest

  1. Open the Pinterest pin with the recipe.
  2. Tap the Share icon (top right of the pin).
  3. Tap More Apps or scroll to find ReciMe.
  4. Select ReciMe. The app opens and imports the recipe automatically.

Option 2 — Paste the Pin URL

  1. Open the pin and tap Share → Copy Link.
  2. Open ReciMe → tap + → Browser.
  3. Paste the Pinterest URL. ReciMe follows the link to the original recipe website automatically.
💡 How it works: Pinterest pins usually link to the original recipe on a food blog or website. ReciMe follows that link and extracts the full structured recipe using the website's data.
