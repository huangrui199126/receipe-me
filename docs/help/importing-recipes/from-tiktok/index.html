Import from TikTok

Importing Recipes · 2 min read

Save recipes from TikTok food videos into your cookbook. ReciMe automatically looks for the original recipe website linked in the description.

Option 1 — Share directly from TikTok

  1. Open the TikTok video with the recipe.
  2. Tap the Share (arrow) button on the right side.
  3. Scroll right through the app icons and tap More.
  4. Find and select ReciMe.
  5. ReciMe opens and tries to find the recipe. If the creator linked to a recipe website, ReciMe imports it automatically.
💡 Tip: Many TikTok food creators link to their website in the video description or profile bio. ReciMe automatically follows these links to get the full recipe.

Option 2 — Copy the description text

  1. Tap the description on the TikTok video to expand it.
  2. Long-press on the text and select Copy.
  3. Open ReciMe → tap + → Paste Text.
  4. Paste the text. ReciMe parses ingredients and steps automatically.
