Import from a recipe website

Importing Recipes · 2 min read

Import recipes from thousands of websites including AllRecipes, Food Network, NYT Cooking, Serious Eats, BBC Good Food, and more.

Steps

  1. Find the recipe on any recipe website in Safari or Chrome.
  2. Tap the browser's Share button.
  3. Select RecipeMe from the share sheet. Or copy the URL and open RecipeMe → + → Browser → paste the URL.
  4. RecipeMe extracts the title, ingredients, instructions, nutrition, and cover photo automatically.
  5. Choose which cookbook to save it to and tap Save Recipe.
✅ Works with: AllRecipes, Food Network, NYT Cooking, Serious Eats, Epicurious, BBC Good Food, Tasty, Delish, SimplyRecipes, and most food blogs that use standard recipe markup.
ℹ️ Doesn't work? Some websites block automated access. Try using Paste Text instead — copy the recipe text from the page and paste it into RecipeMe.
